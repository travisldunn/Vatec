ConvaTec is a global medical products and technologies group focused on therapies for the management of chronic conditions. Our four franchises provide products used for advanced chronic and acute wound care, ostomy care, continence and critical care, and infusion devices used in the treatment of diabetes and other conditions.

Across our operations as a developer, manufacturer and marketer of innovative medical products, ConvaTec has leading market positions in large markets, which are growing at 4-6% per annum, driven by an ageing population, increase in the prevalence of chronic conditions and increased life expectancy of patients suffering from these conditions. In 2015 ConvaTec generated more than 75% of our revenue from products used by patients with chronic care conditions.
